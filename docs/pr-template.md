# PR Description Template

Copy this to `.github/pull_request_template.md` in your repository.
The Review Checkpoint GitHub Action parses the Goal, Constraints, and Risk Areas
sections automatically and posts a structured comment on your PRs.

---

```markdown
## Goal
<!-- What was the agent/you trying to accomplish? Be specific. -->

## Constraints
<!-- What rules or limits apply? What must not break? (optional) -->

## Risk Areas
<!-- Where should reviewers focus? What could go wrong? (optional) -->

## Changes
<!-- Summary of what actually changed, for non-AI-generated context. -->
```
